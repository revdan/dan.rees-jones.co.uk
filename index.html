About Me

Aside from everything internet–related, I’ve also been a professional bass player for ten years, playing bass in everything from covers bands to West End shows. It’s taken me all over the world and I’ve had some fantastic experiences. It’s time to settle down a bit now, though.

I’d consider myself a centre–brained person. I appreciate beautiful design just as much as beautiful code, and I’m equally as happy problem–solving as I am creating. I’m an autodidact, and most of my free time is spent learning something, whether it’s a new skill or language, or just having my mind blown by Wikipedia.

I’m easy to get along with, but very persevering when presented with new challenges to overcome.

I think the most important thing in life is never to lose your sense of imagination and wonder. But it does help to have a finely–tuned sense of the ridiculous.
